# Podders — Product Brief

## One-Line Pitch

Podders continuously reads 50+ crypto and macro channels in Indonesian and English so you always know what matters — not just in the morning, but as it develops.

## User Persona

**Ardi, 28, Jakarta.** Trades crypto (mostly alts + BTC perpetuals) and watches IHSG on the side. Follows 20+ Discord channels, 10 Twitter accounts, and a handful of Telegram alpha groups across two languages. His pain: at any given moment he has hundreds of unread messages, half in Indonesian slang, half in English CT jargon. He skims, misses things, and finds out about a narrative shift from a friend's WhatsApp message three hours too late.

## Always-On Loop

Podders runs 24/7. It delivers intelligence through multiple surfaces depending on urgency:

- **Flash reports** — Pushed via Discord webhook the moment a high-signal event is detected. Ardi's phone buzzes; he reads three sentences and knows whether to act now or ignore.
- **3-hour market pulses** — Scheduled digests that compress the last few hours of chatter into a quick read. Keeps Ardi current without scrolling.
- **Daily digest** — The full overnight summary, delivered at 09:00 WIB. Covers what happened while he slept, what to watch, what changed.
- **Dashboard** — A web UI Ardi can check anytime. Current entity status, sentiment trends, source activity. No refresh needed — the data is always up to date.

The daily digest is one output, not the product. Podders replaces the habit of scrolling channels — not just in the morning, but all day. If Ardi checks his phone, sees a flash report, and adjusts a position before the rest of CT catches on, Podders did its job.

## Three Questions Podders Answers

1. **What just happened?** — Continuous summary across all sources, both languages, ranked by signal. Surfaces in flash reports, pulses, and the dashboard as events develop.
2. **What should I be watching?** — Entities with rising mention velocity, sentiment shifts, or cross-source correlation. Updated every cycle, not once a day.
3. **What changed?** — Sentiment deltas, new narratives emerging, old narratives fading. Tracked over hours and days, visible on the dashboard and in every digest.

## Why Podders Exists

The coverage problem: no human can read 50 channels in two languages around the clock. You either spend hours scrolling or you miss things. Ardi picks 5-6 channels to skim and hopes the important stuff surfaces. It usually does not — and by the time it does, he is late.

Podders watches everything, all the time. It reads Indonesian Discord alpha groups AND English CT AND macro news AND local crypto communities. It cross-references across sources and languages. It compresses thousands of messages into flash reports, periodic pulses, and daily digests — each taking 30 seconds to read — and keeps a live dashboard for when Ardi wants to dig deeper.

The value is not AI summarization. The value is continuous coverage at inhuman scale with cross-source verification, delivered at the speed the market moves.

## Why Indonesian

This is the moat, not a feature.

- No credible Indonesian-language crypto intelligence product exists.
- Indonesia has 20M+ crypto holders, a growing tradfi retail base, and zero tools that process Bahasa content.
- Indonesian crypto communities run on Discord and Telegram in Bahasa. English-only tools miss this entirely.
- Podders processes both languages natively: Indonesian input, English output. The bilingual pipeline is the product.
- A US-based competitor would need to build Indonesian language processing from scratch. They will not bother for a market they do not understand.

## What Podders Is NOT

- **Not a trading bot.** Podders surfaces conditions. It does not execute trades or output buy/sell signals.
- **Not on-chain analytics.** No wallet tracking, no DEX volume charts, no token flow analysis. That is Nansen/Arkham territory.
- **Not a portfolio tracker.** No PnL, no holdings dashboard, no tax reports.
- **Not a general-purpose AI assistant.** The chat feature answers questions grounded in your actual data — summaries, reports, items. It does not answer general questions, speculate, or produce anything not backed by retrieved context. If Podders does not have data on a topic, it says so.

## Success Metric

**Behavioral change**: Ardi opens TradingView and watches something he would not have watched without Podders. He stops scrolling Discord and Telegram because Podders already covered it — not just in the morning, but throughout the day.

Measurable proxies: daily digest open rate above 80% over 30 days; flash report click-through within 5 minutes of delivery; dashboard sessions per week. If Ardi checks Podders before he checks Discord, the product is working.
